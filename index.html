SZA: the R'n'B superstar in concert in Paris in June

Kendrick Lamar, Doja Cat, Cardi B, and Rihanna’s collaborator has become one of the most respected R’n’B artists in three EPs and two albums. Celebrated by both the music press and the public, the singer SZA will perform at the Accor Arena in Paris, on Monday, June 5th.SZA will perform songs from her new album SOS and her latest single Saturn.It is rumoured that she might perform with some of her featured artists like Drake Summer Walker and Travis Scott.
